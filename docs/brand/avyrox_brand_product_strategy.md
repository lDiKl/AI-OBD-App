# Avyrox Brand & Product Strategy

Date: 2026-03-05

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. App Store & Google Play Name Availability Strategy

Before publishing mobile apps, it is important to verify that the
application name **Avyrox** is available in major app stores.

## Stores to check

Primary platforms:

-   Google Play Store
-   Apple App Store

Secondary platforms:

-   Huawei AppGallery
-   Samsung Galaxy Store

## Recommended app names

Instead of publishing an app simply as "Avyrox", it is better to use the
product names.

Driver app:

**Avyrox Drive**\
AI‑powered vehicle diagnostics and service finder

Service station app:

**Avyrox Service**\
Management platform for car repair shops

These names are significantly easier to register because generic
single‑word names are often already taken.

## Suggested Store Titles

Example Play Store titles:

Avyrox Drive -- Car Diagnostics & Service Finder

Avyrox Service -- Workshop Management

This improves:

-   discoverability
-   SEO inside app stores
-   keyword ranking

## Suggested Package Names

Android package examples:

com.avyrox.drive\
com.avyrox.service\
com.avyrox.cloud

# 2. Brand Guide

## Brand Name

**Avyrox**

Short, technology‑focused, and scalable.

Pronunciation:

AV‑ee‑roks

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Brand Positioning

Avyrox is a platform connecting **drivers and automotive service
businesses** through diagnostics, AI, and cloud infrastructure.

Core idea:

**The intelligent automotive service network**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Brand Personality

The brand should communicate:

-   innovation
-   reliability
-   automotive expertise
-   simplicity

Comparable brand feeling:

modern SaaS companies such as Stripe or Linear.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Visual Identity

Recommended direction:

Primary color: Deep blue (#1E3A8A)

Secondary color: Electric blue (#3B82F6)

Accent: Light cyan (#22D3EE)

Neutral colors:

-   Dark gray (#111827)
-   Light gray (#F3F4F6)

These colors work well for:

-   dashboards
-   automotive UI
-   SaaS interfaces

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Logo Concept

Possible ideas:

1.  Stylized **A** shaped like a road
2.  Abstract **sensor / signal icon** representing vehicle diagnostics
3.  Minimal geometric tech logo

Logo style:

-   simple
-   geometric
-   scalable
-   recognizable in small app icons

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Brand Slogan Ideas

Possible slogans:

-   Intelligent car service platform
-   Smarter vehicle diagnostics
-   The future of automotive service
-   Connect your car to the right service

# 3. Product Architecture

The Avyrox ecosystem should be built as a **platform**, not just a
single app.

Main brand:

Avyrox

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Core Products

### Avyrox Drive

Mobile application for drivers.

Features:

-   OBD diagnostics
-   vehicle health monitoring
-   error explanation
-   service station search
-   repair estimates

Target users:

car owners

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Avyrox Service

Business platform for service stations.

Features:

-   workshop CRM
-   repair estimates
-   diagnostics integration
-   customer management
-   scheduling

Target users:

car repair businesses

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Avyrox Cloud

Backend platform powering the ecosystem.

Features:

-   diagnostics database
-   AI analysis
-   service marketplace
-   API infrastructure

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Avyrox
